How It Works

A simple and seamless process to share your books.

1. List Your Books

Easily add books you own but no longer need. Add details like condition and a short description.

2. Find What You Want

Browse the global library or search for specific titles, authors, and genres that interest you.

3. Request an Exchange

Send a request to the book's owner. Once accepted, arrange the exchange and enjoy your new read!

Serverless Architecture

This entire application runs on the client side without a traditional backend. All data, including users, books, and exchange requests, is managed via a centralized engine utilizing the browser's LocalStorage API. It perfectly demonstrates modular Vanilla JavaScript and modern responsive styling.
